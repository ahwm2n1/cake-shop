Meet Mums Bakery

It all started in a small home kitchen in Mirpur, where a passion for baking turned into a dream. Mums Bakery, armed with her grandmother's recipes and a creative vision, began crafting cakes that weren't just desserts - they were edible art.

What began as birthday cakes for friends and family quickly grew into a beloved local bakery. Each cake tells a story, each dessert carries a memory, and every creation is made with the same love and attention that started this journey.

Today, Cakeland Bakers stands as a testament to what happens when passion meets perseverance. We've baked for thousands of celebrations, from intimate birthdays to grand weddings, always keeping true to our core values: quality, creativity, and heartfelt service.
