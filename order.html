Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order? +
For simple designs: 2-3 days. For custom/wedding cakes: 1-2 weeks. For large events: 3-4 weeks minimum.
Do you offer delivery? +
Yes! Free delivery within Mirpur. Delivery charges apply for other areas in Islamabad. We also offer pickup.
Can I customize flavors? +
Absolutely! We offer chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, carrot, lemon, and custom flavor combinations.
What payment methods do you accept? +
Cash on delivery, bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa. 50% advance for large/wedding orders.
Do you make eggless cakes? +
Yes, we offer eggless options. Please mention this in the special instructions when ordering.
How are cakes priced? +
Price depends on size, design complexity, and ingredients. Basic cakes start from Rs. 2,500.
